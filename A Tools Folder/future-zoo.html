Ideation · ReWilded

What could the zoo experience look like in 50 to 100 years?

The Rewilded Park

Welcome to the zoo of the future, where instead of rushing to see every animal in a day, we slow down to experience a single species, deeply.

Giraffe Hub · Entrance

Each encounter begins at its own entrance, just off a central rewilded park. Today you are entering the giraffe hub, a space for hands-on, species-specific, sensory learning.

Treehouse Café · Rooftop

Up the stairs we find rooftop dining offering adult-friendly, awe-filled encounters, as giraffes pass by without a script.

Kinetic Playground · Outdoors

Beyond the main building, young explorers engage in kinetic learning, with ropes courses and play designed to deepen species understanding.

Central Park · Savannah

Wandering deeper, the back of each module opens to natural terrain. Today, it's giraffes grazing across the savannah. Next time, you might choose the gorilla hub. Or a majestic encounter with our elephants.

This is no checklist zoo. This is reverence, ReWilded.
