Research

Understanding needs before shaping solutions.

A targeted 10-question survey was crafted to better understand the client's specific requirements. The survey covered crucial aspects such as primary competitors, user demographics, and essential features like honeypots and bot blockers.

This meticulous approach to gathering information laid a solid foundation for understanding the client's needs and shaping the project strategy. The research surfaced key requirements around member management, payment processing, and scalable entry systems that would define the technical architecture.

By identifying user demographics early, the team could anticipate interaction patterns and design for the specific behaviors of existing members, industry participants, and new annual entrants — each with distinct navigation needs and goals.

Key Finding

The survey revealed that users fell into three distinct audiences — each with fundamentally different goals when visiting the site. Existing members needed account management. Industry participants sought event information. New entrants needed a clear registration path.

Designing a single navigation system that served all three without compromise became the central architecture challenge.

Approach

From discovery to strategy to design.

The project began with a collaborative discovery process, blending discussions and independent research to define its scope. Ongoing communication and research played pivotal roles in maintaining a comprehensive understanding throughout the engagement.

01 — Architecture

Intelligent Navigation

Creating a logical information architecture for smooth navigation among existing members, industry participants, and accommodating the expansion of annual entries. Each audience received tailored pathways without fragmenting the overall experience.

02 — Experience

Intuitive User Flows

Crafting an intuitive user experience that accentuates the organization's reputation while resonating effectively with the target audience. Every interaction was designed for clarity and minimal friction.

03 — Integration

Seamless Systems

Developing a system integrating seamlessly with the current member management module (MemberPress) and eCommerce solution (Auth.net/WooCommerce), ensuring existing workflows remained uninterrupted while gaining new capabilities.

04 — Registration

Effortless Submissions

Engineering a solution for entrants to manage registrations and submissions effortlessly. The self-service system reduced administrative overhead while giving entrants full visibility into their submission status.

Solution

Desktop and mobile, unified.

A comprehensive solution was crafted through the design and development of both desktop and mobile applications, aligning with the client's preferences and organizational identity.

The outcome materialized as a succinct Strategy Brief outlining creative and communicative goals. After thorough review, revisions, and final approval, the project seamlessly transitioned into the design phase — where all research insights were synthesized into a cohesive visual and functional system.

The final deliverable blended all elements into a design concept that not only accentuated the organization's reputation but also ensured a meaningful and user-friendly interaction across every touchpoint.

Design Principle

The design ensures consistent engagement was maintained as a project-wide necessity — not an afterthought. Every design decision traced back to the strategy brief.

By keeping communication ongoing throughout, the solution remained aligned with evolving client needs rather than locked to initial assumptions.
