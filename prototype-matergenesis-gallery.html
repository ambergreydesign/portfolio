Visions of MaterGenesis
AI-generated imagery across Art, Ecology, and Religion — MidJourney, 2023
Step 1
Literature Review
Research into matriarchal societies, ecology, belief systems, and creative expression formed the knowledge base.
Step 2
Concept Map
A branching map with "Matriarchal World" at center. Each branch produced sub-categories and prompt-ready details.
Step 3
ChatGPT Prompts
ChatGPT was taught to construct MidJourney prompts from each map branch, using IMAGE | STYLE | MOOD | SCENE structure.
Step 4
MidJourney Output
Each prompt generated 4 images. Individual crops were selected and assembled in Photoshop for the final collection.
Every image below traces back to a concept map branch, through a ChatGPT-refined prompt, to a MidJourney visualization. Prompts were iteratively refined to minimize AI bias and ensure alignment with the project's core themes.
